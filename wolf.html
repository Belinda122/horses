Wolf (Canis lupus)

A canine with thick fur that comes in various colours like brown, grey, black, or a mixture. They are native to North America and Eurasia. Wolves are known to travel in packs and have complex social structures.

Connections

The relationship between wolves and horses is an example of predation, where one party benefits while the other dies. Wolves primarily hunt hooved mammals and excel at cooperative hunting practices.

Phylum: Chordata Class: Mammalia Order: Carnivora Family: Canidae Genus: Canis
