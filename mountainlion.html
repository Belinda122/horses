Mountain Lion (Puma concolor)

A large feline with a beige coat. As an adaptable species, it can be found all over the Americas in diverse environments.

Connections

The relationship between mountain lions and horses is an example of predation, where one party benefits while the other dies. Horses are a source of food for mountain lions.

Phylum: Chordata Class: Mammalia Order: Carnivora Family: Felidae Genus: Puma
