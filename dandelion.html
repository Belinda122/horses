Dandelion (Taraxacum officinale)

A flowering perennial plant with a distinctive yellow flower head. It is extremely tolerant and propagates easily. As such, it can be found all over the world.

Connections

The relationship between dandelions and horses is an example of mutualism. Horses can consume dandelions as a source of nutrients, and in doing so can aid in seed dispersal through fecal matter.

Phylum: Tracheophyta Class: Magnoliopsida Order: Asterales Family: Asteraceae Genus: Taraxacum
